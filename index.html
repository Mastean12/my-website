Website Design & Development Course

Web development encompasses a wide range of activities involved
in creating and maintaining websites on the internet. It involves
the process of designing, building, and maintaining web
pages or entire websites. Web developers use a variety of
programming languages, frameworks, and tools to bring the visual and
interactive aspects of a website to life. This includes both
front-end development, responsible for the user interface and user
experience, and back-end development, which involves server-side
scripting and database management. Additionally, web development often
involves collaboration between multidisciplinary teams, including
designers, developers, and project managers, to
ensure the seamless functionality and aesthetic appeal
of the final product. With the ever-evolving landscape
of web technologies, staying abreast of the latest
trends and best practices is crucial for web developers to
create innovative and user-friendly online experiences.
